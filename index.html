Format

  • 5 players, 6 rounds — 2v1v1v1 Team Battle
  • 3 Stocks, 10-Min Limit, No Items, Team Attack ON
  • Steven ("The Bachelor") is always on the team of 2
  • The other 3 are "Solo Assassins"

Time Out Rules

If the 10-minute timer expires, winner is determined by official Smash rules:

  • Highest Stock Count — whoever has the most lives left wins
  • Lowest Percentage — if stocks are tied, lowest damage % wins
  • The Team Factor — if Steven or his wingman leads in stocks/%, the Team gets +5. If a Solo Assassin leads, they get +5

Phase 1: The Draft (Rounds 1-3)

  • 3 rounds: each player picks a pool of 3 characters
  • Same order every round: Steven → Rodney → Franco → Ben → Cory
  • Round 1: Steven picks his first teammate (not Cory)
  • Rounds 2-3: Steven pairs with whoever placed 5th last round
  • Must use drafted characters only

Phase 2: Reverse Selection (Rounds 4-5)

  • Any character allowed — draft pool discarded
  • Steven pairs with whoever placed 5th last round
  • Character draft resumes in inverse score order (lowest picks first)
  • Tiebreaker: whoever died earliest in the previous round picks first
  • Only Steven may play the same character twice — all others must pick unique

Phase 3: The Finale (Round 6)

  • 1v1 between the top two point leaders after 5 rounds
  • Free character choice, draft order by lowest score
  • Tiebreaker: whoever died earliest in previous rounds picks first
  • The highest scorer must random select their character

Scoring

  • Team Win (Steven + Wingman each): +5 pts
  • Solo Win (Assassin beats everyone): +5 pts
  • Runner-Up (2nd place): +2 pts
  • First Blood (first KO of the round): +2 pts
  • Groom-Slayer (take Steven's last stock): +1 pt
  • Cory-Slayer (take any of Cory's stocks): +1 pt per stock, +3 for final stock
  • Spiked (kill by spike/meteor KO): -1 pt for the killer — does not apply to final stock kills (maximum disrespect allowed for match enders)

Stakes & Punishments

Last Place (5th) — The Guerneville Asshole 5th place must dunk their head underwater AND replenish beverages for the group until someone else takes last.
The Cory Debuff When Cory wins Rounds 1-3, he must take a "Victory Shot" to naturally handicap his power.
